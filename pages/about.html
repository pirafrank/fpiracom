I'm a 22yo Web Developer and a Computer Engineering student at University of Catania, Italy. I'm a curious mind striving for more.

During the last two years I joined EESTEC, became president of the local committee and coordinator of the international IT team. I got in touch with the local startup scene and joined Youthub Catania, an association focused on matching people's skills and organising startup related events (like the Startup Weekend - Change Makers, held in Syracuse last May).
