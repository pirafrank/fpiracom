Chat to someone without adding his/her mobile number to your contacts.
Works with iOS and Android apps as well as web version.

Type in mobile number of the person you want to chat with.
Only numbers. Country prefix required, without '+' sign (e.g. +393160909123).

Go

How it works

  • Nothing special, it uses WhatsApp Click-to-chat feature.
  • It actually just opens an URL in another tab. The mobile is part of that URL.
  • String concatenation happens on your browser. Your input won't be visible to me or any third part (other than WhatsApp itself) in any way.
